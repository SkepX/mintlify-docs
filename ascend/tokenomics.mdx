## ASCEND Utility

$ASCEND is the native utility token powering Ascend Markets, an event perpetuals platform.

It plays a central role in aligning traders, liquidity providers, and long term protocol participants.

---

## Key Utilities

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Collateral" icon="coins">
    ASCEND can be posted as **additional collateral** across all markets.

    Benefits include discounted trading fees, access to higher effective leverage, and priority participation in new markets.

    During liquidation, ASCEND collateral may be **burned**, introducing a deflationary mechanism tied to protocol risk.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Staking" icon="piggy-bank">
    Stake ASCEND to earn a **share of protocol fees**, including trading fees, funding fees, and future premium market fees.

    Staking aligns long-term holders with the growth and success of the Ascend ecosystem.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Fee Discounts" icon="percent">
    Holding ASCEND unlocks **discounted trading fees** across all prediction and event markets.

    Discounts can be stacked with collateral-based discounts, rewarding active participation and long-term holding.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Governance" icon="landmark">
    ASCEND holders can **vote on protocol decisions**, including upgrades, new market launches, risk parameters, and treasury allocation.

    Governance ensures Ascend evolves in line with its community.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
